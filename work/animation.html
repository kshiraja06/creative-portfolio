ANIMATIC

Traces of the Past, Tides of the Future is an animatic about the commodification of culture, told through a personal, slightly fragmented story. It follows Maria, a young Goan gift-shop owner, whose normal day slowly shifts as she experiences two versions of Goa, what it was and what it is becoming. Through this, she begins to see what tourism has changed and what it has taken. The project looks at that tension and what it means to hold on to something that is slowly being reshaped.
