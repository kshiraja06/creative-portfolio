how stars are born!

a visualization of the first five pages of the little book of big history. this project exercised my skills on abstraction, visualization and decision making. we were tasked with selecting relevant information from the text and discarding what we deemed unnecessary. conceptualizing big (pun intended) topics in science to be understood by a layman, yet doing justice to the immensity of how marvelous these phenomenons are was a steep learning curve in this project.
